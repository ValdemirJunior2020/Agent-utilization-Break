# AI-Powered Operations Ideas Built Around This App

These are leadership-focused ideas designed for your Agent Utilization and Break Time Compliance workflow.

1. **Repeated offender detection**  
   Rank agents by how many times they exceed 60 minutes across 1-day and 7-day reports.

2. **Critical risk score**  
   Score agents by frequency, max break time, and whether they appear in multiple uploads.

3. **Weekly improvement score**  
   Compare each call center against the prior week: fewer agents above 60 minutes = improvement.

4. **Exception tracking**  
   Force visible exception reasons for agents with valid laws, new-hire training, training leaders, or non-phone roles.

5. **Leadership summary generator**  
   Auto-generate a clean message showing reviewed agents, above-60 count, critical count, and call center ranking.

6. **What changed this week?**  
   Show new offenders, resolved offenders, repeat offenders, and worsening agents.

7. **Coaching queue**  
   Build a prioritized list of agents needing coaching based on repeated issues and critical break times.

8. **Center risk score**  
   Rank each call center by issue volume, critical rate, repeated agents, and missing exception explanations.

9. **Upload quality warnings**  
   Warn when a file has no date, no HP IDs, missing exception column, or mismatched call center name.

10. **One-click call center email**  
   Generate a ready-to-send follow-up message to each center with the exact agents and dates needing action.

11. **Trend prediction**  
   Once there is enough history, predict which centers are likely to miss compliance next week.

12. **Audit accountability**  
   Show who edited, deleted, restored, or changed thresholds, with previous and new values.
